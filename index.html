Overview of Golf

Golf is a sport where the goal is to hit a small ball into a series of holes using as few swings as possible. It’s played on big, open courses with different challenges like sand pits, water, and sloping greens. Every hole feels like a new puzzle, and players use different clubs depending on the shot they need. The game started in Scotland hundreds of years ago and has grown into a worldwide pastime, enjoyed by both casual players and pros. Golf is a mix of skill, strategy, and patience, and it’s as much about enjoying the outdoors as it is about the competition. Whether you’re out for fun or chasing a perfect score, it’s a game that can hook you for life.
